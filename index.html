Peter Baker

Once a Twin Cities scenic artist - no more.

Previously a scenic painter, I'm now studying to be a full-time web developer.


My work has primarily been for theatrical productions, but my interests span all realms of art and design. The past seven years of professional scenic work have helped me develop skills in creating and adapting designs with particular audiences and user communities in mind.


Feel free to contact me with questions or for more information at any time.
